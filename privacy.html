Privacy Policy

Effective date: today · Updated as needed

Meimonde is built on a simple promise: your photos never leave your device. Everything is stored locally in your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data through the photobooth itself.

1. What data we collect

The Meimonde Photo Booth application collects no data through the application itself. Specifically:

  • All photos captured in the booth are stored in your browser's localStorage and never transmitted to any server.
  • We do not use cookies, analytics scripts, or tracking pixels.
  • We do not use any third-party advertising or data brokers.

2. Camera access

The photobooth requests access to your device's camera via the browser's getUserMedia API. This access is:

  • Local only — the camera stream is never transmitted outside your browser.
  • Used solely for the photobooth capture feature.
  • Revocable at any time via your browser's settings.

3. Photo submissions (Send Us Your Pictures)

If you choose to submit photos for our community gallery via the menu form, you are voluntarily sharing:

  • Your name and optional social media handle
  • Your email address (for curation approval communication)
  • A caption and the photo(s) you attach

Submissions are sent to meitongkimnyp@gmail.com via your own email client. Meimonde uses this information solely to review and potentially feature your photo on the community library page.

We will never share your email or personal details with third parties. You may request removal of your submission at any time by emailing meitongkimnyp@gmail.com.

4. Reviews

Reviews submitted through the Write a Review feature are sent via your email client to Meimonde. We may feature anonymised excerpts of reviews on the website. We will not share your name or email publicly without your consent.

5. Spotify integration (CD Studio)

The CD Studio feature allows you to enter a Spotify playlist URL, which may load a Spotify embed widget. This is governed by Spotify's Privacy Policy. Meimonde does not store or transmit your Spotify URL.

6. Children's privacy

Meimonde is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

7. Your rights

Since we do not collect personal data through the application itself, there is no data to request, rectify, or delete on our servers. For any data shared via email (submissions, reviews), you may contact us at meitongkimnyp@gmail.com to request deletion.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above will reflect any changes. Continued use of Meimonde after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions? Reach out at meitongkimnyp@gmail.com or connect on LinkedIn.
